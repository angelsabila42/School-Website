ABOUT US

Our History

Gayaza high school was founded by the Christian missionaries who belonged to the Church missionary society in 1905 on a land donated to the Church by Kabaka Daudi Chwa II.
The school started with a population of 4 sudents being the first Uganda's girl's boarding school.
The school was established on a 140-acres of land and its motto was "banno" meaning "your friends".
It was in 1939, that the informal senior secondary school began and later in 1962, the school was officially separated i.e,into the junior and the senior schools . From 4 girls, the twin Schools have gone on to become one of the Uganda's giant schools with an enrollment of more than 1000 pupils and students respectively.

Us Today

Gayaza high school is still a Church of Uganda founded and a Gorvenment aided girls' boarding school with both O and A level, offering both Arts and Sciences with a number of co-curricular activities that include;

Our management

Gayaza high school is headed by a head teacher (Currently Mrs Kizito Robinah Katongole) who is supported by two deputies and a director of studies.
We have three deans ie Dean for lower secondary(S1-S2), Dean for middle secondary(S3-S4), and Dean for upper School(S5-S6).
A senior man and a senior woman also support the school affairs regarding the student affairs.

Our location
